    How It Works

    A four-stage engineering pipeline from raw scan to manifold mesh.

    01

    Ingest Raw E57

    PyE57 reads every scan with ignore_missing_fields — resilient to missing intensity or RGB.

    02

    AI Noise Filtering

    Scikit-learn Isolation Forest removes statistical outliers before downsampling.

    03

    Ball Pivoting Reconstruction

    Open3D BPA with adaptive multi-radius balls fitted to local point density.

    04

    Manifold Optimization

    Degenerate triangle cleanup + Taubin smoothing for print-ready topology.

    Technical Features
